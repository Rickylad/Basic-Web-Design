Information Technology has revolutionized our lives in different aspects. It is in education, business, communication and medicine etc. Several decades ago, we learned knowledge in a traditional way. We brought books and went to school every weekday.
However, today we can read books with a laptop or a smartphone. Even in a remote area, students can get access to knowledge through online courses.
It takes over a week or longer to receive a letter in the past, but today anyone can send and receive a message or an email immediately. With the development of IT, our lives will be easier, more convenient and more efficient.
